fourth-year computer science and physics student at the university of pennsylvania. san francisco bay area native and travel enthusiast

I am currently a senior at the University of Pennsylvania persuing a double major in computer science and Physics (B.S.E) with a concentration in Business (Wharton School) and minors in mathematics and data science. I am also concurrently persuing an accelerated masters in computer science (M.S.E) from the school of engineering and applied sciences.

I have extensive research and industry experience, having applied ML models on the FAERS drug data in the Shen Lab at the Perelman School of Medicine and have worked on classified projects as a research intern for Intrinsyx technologies at NASA Ames Research Center.
When I am not in the lab or grinding psets, you can find me at a hackathon, surfing, or even travelling the world, attempting to achieve my goal of hitting a hundred countries by 2030!
